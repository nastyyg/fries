Nasty YG

Realf D Twalla [1] (born 22 July 2003), known professionally as Nasty YG, is a Liberian rapper, songwriter, graphic designer, computer programmer, soccer player and record producer. After the released track that he was featured on title (Woman Baynay) with Yung Text, El Shine & Classic K (Thugboi) during the year 2017 the song became to hit in some areas in Monrovia, Liberia. And Nasty YG became an "Undisputed" and Famous Kid in his school, ‘St Mary’s Catholic High School. He became famous and decided to do music; (Woman Baynay) was his first track ever in his life. Since the year 2017. Realf was later featured again on an afro beats track title Junky Boy by Kizz_Beneficial in (2018), Nasty YG continue his love for music and expressed his feelings of how much he love music, but many people taught he was rapping for his girlfriend.. During the year (2019) Nasty YG started to write his debut album tracks but something happened.[2] He was the having a lot of records opportunities with some big artist in his country, but unfortunately his Dad was not happy with him doing music, so he told him to quit music.
